Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Digntag provides customized digital design services. Because work is started immediately after an order is confirmed, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis as described below.

Cancellation

  • If you cancel before work begins, you may be eligible for a full refund.
  • If you cancel after work begins, you may be eligible for a partial refund based on work completed.
  • If the final design/invitation is delivered, refunds are generally not available.

Revisions

We aim to deliver high-quality work. If you are not satisfied, please contact us for revisions or fixes. Refunds will be considered if we are unable to provide the agreed deliverable.

How to request a refund

To request a refund, contact us on WhatsApp with your order details and reason: +91 8882816805

Processing time

Approved refunds are processed within a reasonable timeframe. The time for the refund to reflect in your account may depend on your bank/payment provider.
