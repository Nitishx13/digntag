Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Digntag ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website and services (the "Service").

Information we collect

When you create or request an invitation or event page, you may provide details such as your name, phone number, email, event details, images/videos, and messages.

Guest and RSVP information

If you use RSVP tracking, we may collect guest responses and any custom fields you configure (for example: meal preference, number of attendees, notes).

Usage and device information

We may collect basic analytics and technical information such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and approximate location derived from IP.

How we use information

  • To provide and improve the Service (create invitations, RSVP pages, event links).

  • To prevent abuse, fraud, and security issues.
  • To comply with legal requirements.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the Service (for example: hosting, analytics, messaging tools), or when required by law.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Service and for legitimate business purposes (for example: dispute resolution and enforcing agreements), unless a longer period is required by law.

Your choices
